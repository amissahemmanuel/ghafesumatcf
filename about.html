We Are GHAFES UMaT-CF

GHAFES UMaT-CF is a vibrant Christian fellowship dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth, developing Christlike character, promoting academic excellence, and building relationships that reflect God's love.

Our Vision

A movement of Christlike Students and Associates proclaiming Christ, transforming campuses, the church, and society.

Our Mission

To reach, equip, and connect students and associates to be effective witnesses of Jesus Christ and agents of change.

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit..."

Matthew 28:19-20 (ESV)

What We Believe

God

We believe in one true God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—eternal, sovereign, loving, and just.

Man

Man was created in God's image, fell into sin, and can be restored through Christ.

Scripture

The Bible is divinely inspired and authoritative for all matters of faith and practice.

Jesus Christ

Jesus is both God and man. Through His life, death, and resurrection, He brings salvation.

The Holy Spirit

The Spirit convicts, regenerates, empowers, and gifts believers for service.

The Church

The Church is God's instrument for disciple-making, worship, and transformation of society.

Our Core Values

Faithfulness to Scripture

We are committed to upholding and living by the truth of God's Word, using it as our guide in all aspects of life and ministry.

Prayer

We maintain a strong emphasis on prayer as the foundation of our relationship with God and the source of our spiritual power.

Integrity

We strive to maintain honesty, transparency, and moral uprightness in all our actions and relationships.

Stewardship

We are committed to responsible management of all resources entrusted to us, including time, talents, and finances.

Pursuit of Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our endeavors, whether in academic pursuits, ministry, or personal development.

Love of God and Others

We demonstrate God's love through our actions and relationships, showing care and compassion to all people.
