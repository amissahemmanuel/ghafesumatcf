Connect Groups

Our Connect Groups, Christlike discipleship Family(CDF) are small gatherings of students who meet regularly to study God's Word, pray together, and support one another in their spiritual journey. These groups provide a safe space for meaningful relationships, personal growth, and accountability within our fellowship.

Our CDF are organized by halls of residence to make it easier for students to participate. Each group is led by trained student leaders who facilitate discussions and ensure everyone feels welcome and included. Through these groups, you'll find a community where you can grow in your faith, share your struggles, and celebrate victories together.


Join a CDF Group.

We'd love to help you find a CDF Group that's right for you. Please fill out the form below with your details, and we'll help you connect with a CDF group that matches your schedule and location. Our CDF Groups meet at various times throughout the week, and we'll do our best to accommodate your preferences.
