Join the Team

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a difference in the lives of students at UMaT. Volunteering with GHAFES UMaT-CF is a great way to serve God, develop your leadership skills, and build meaningful relationships with other believers. We have various ministry opportunities that match different gifts and interests.

As a volunteer, you'll be part of a supportive community that's committed to making a positive impact on campus. Whether you're interested in worship, teaching, outreach, or administration, there's a place for you to serve and grow. We provide training and support to help you succeed in your ministry role.


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering Team.

Help create a welcoming and safe environment for our fellowship meetings and events. Responsibilities include greeting attendees, managing seating arrangements, and ensuring the security of our gatherings. This ministry is crucial for making visitors feel welcome and maintaining order during our services.

Child Evangelism Team.

Make a difference in the lives of children by teaching them about God's love through Bible stories, songs, and fun activities. This ministry helps children develop a strong foundation in their faith while providing a safe and engaging environment for them to learn and grow.

Music Team.

Use your musical talents to lead worship and create an atmosphere of praise. Whether you play an instrument, sing, or have technical skills, there's a place for you in our music ministry. Help create meaningful worship experiences that draw people closer to God.

Prayer Team.

Join our dedicated prayer warriors in interceding for the fellowship, campus, and individual needs. This ministry plays a vital role in supporting our activities through prayer and helping members grow in their prayer life. Be part of a team that brings God's power and presence into every aspect of our ministry.

Administrative Support.

Help keep our fellowship running smoothly by assisting with various administrative tasks. This includes managing records, coordinating communications, and supporting event planning. Your organizational skills can help ensure that our ministry operates efficiently and effectively.

Media Team.

Use your creative talents to enhance our worship services and events through visual design, stage setup, and multimedia presentations. Help create an engaging atmosphere that supports our message and ministry goals.

Technical Team.

Ensure smooth operation of our services and events by managing sound, lighting, and technical equipment. This ministry is essential for creating a professional and distraction-free environment for worship and teaching.

Communications.

Help spread the word about our fellowship and events through various communication channels. This includes managing social media, creating content, and ensuring effective communication with members and the campus community.
