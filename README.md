# GHAFES UMaT-CF Website

This is the official website for the Ghana Fellowship of Evangelical Students (GHAFES) at the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) Christian Fellowship.

## Overview

The GHAFES UMaT-CF website serves as a digital platform for the Christian fellowship, providing information about events, services, and ways to get involved in the campus ministry. The website is designed to be responsive and user-friendly, making it accessible across various devices.

## Features

- **Home Page**: Showcases the fellowship's mission and upcoming events
- **About Section**: Information about GHAFES UMaT-CF and its activities
- **Events Calendar**: Details about upcoming events and meetings
- **Connect Groups**: Information about joining small groups
- **Volunteer Opportunities**: Ways to get involved in the ministry
- **Contact Information**: Easy access to location and contact details

## Regular Activities

- **Main Service**: Wednesdays and Saturdays at 6:30 PM
- **Child Evangelism**: Saturdays at 3:00 PM
- **Prayer Meetings**:
  - Mondays at 7:00 PM
  - Wednesdays at 5:00 AM (via WhatsApp)
